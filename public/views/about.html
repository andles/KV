About KV

Built on a Tradition of Excellence



KV Electric has been in business since 1984, and for the past 31 years we have strived to excel in all aspects of the electrical industry. Whether we're doing large or small scale commercial, industrial, residential or renewable construction, KV Electric will push for superior service and customer satisfaction.

Although we are promoting solar power and NetMetering, KV Electric has been a premier Electrical Contractor in the Southern Utah region. No matter what your electrical needs may be, we will exceed your expectations.

"Our mission at KV Electric is to provide our customers with superior quality of service and products and competitive costs. We strive to maintain customer satisfaction and confidence, and to pursue excellence, innovation and improvement in all aspects of our trade." - Kerry Johnson, President
